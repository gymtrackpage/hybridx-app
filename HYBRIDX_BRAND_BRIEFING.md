# HYBRIDX Brand & Product Briefing
## Context Document for AI Marketing & Email Tool

## 1. Company Overview

**Brand Name:** HYBRIDX (stylised as HYBRIDX.CLUB)

HYBRIDX is an AI-powered training platform built specifically for HYROX competitors and hybrid fitness athletes. The company's mission is to give every HYROX competitor access to intelligent, personalised coaching — acting as an always-on training partner that understands the unique demands of hybrid endurance and functional fitness racing.

HYBRIDX sits at the intersection of high-performance sports coaching and consumer technology, delivering structured training plans, real-time AI coaching, and analytics through a mobile-first application available on iOS, Android, and web.

---

## 2. What is HYROX?

HYROX is a global fitness racing competition that combines 8km of running with 8 functional fitness stations (such as sled pushes, rowing, burpee broad jumps, and sandbag lunges). It is one of the fastest-growing fitness sports in the world, attracting both recreational athletes and elite competitors. Events are held worldwide and competitors can race individually, as doubles, or in relay teams.

HYROX training requires a specific blend of:
- Aerobic endurance (running capacity and pacing)
- Functional strength (for the 8 obstacle stations)
- "Compromised running" — the ability to run efficiently while fatigued after strength work
- Race strategy and pacing intelligence

This is what makes HYROX uniquely challenging and why generic training apps fall short — HYBRIDX is purpose-built for it.

## 3. The HYBRIDX App — Core Product

**Tagline:** *"Your AI-powered partner for peak HYROX performance"*

The HYBRIDX app is the company's primary product. It is a structured training and coaching platform delivered as a native mobile app (iOS & Android via Capacitor) and a Progressive Web App.

### Key Features

**Structured Training Programs**
Six carefully designed 12-week programs tailored to different goals and experience levels:
- *First Steps to Hyrox* — Beginner-focused, 4 days/week, gym-experienced athletes new to HYROX
- *Hyrox Fusion Balance* — Intermediate, balanced run/strength approach, 4 days/week
- *Hyrox Run Performance* — Intermediate–Advanced, run-heavy focus, 5 days/week
- *Hyrox Doubles & Relay Prep* — Specialised for partner and team events, 4 days/week
- *Olympic Lifting & Power Cycle* — Strength specialisation with HYROX integration, 4 days/week
- *Ultra Elite Performance* — Advanced competitive prep, 6 days/week

**AI Coaching (The "Edge Coach")**
HYBRIDX embeds AI throughout the training experience:
- Personalised motivational coaching based on individual workout history
- AI Training Assistant answering questions on technique, nutrition, race strategy, and recovery
- AI-generated daily dashboard summaries and workout-specific tips
- Smart push notifications with AI-generated messages to drive consistency
- On-demand custom workout generation for athletes without a fixed program
- AI-generated training articles and educational content

**Workout Tracking & Analytics**
- Daily workout logging with structured sessions
- Weekly consistency tracking with visual charts
- Training streak monitoring
- Session history and progress visualisation
- Pace zone calculations for running training

**Strava Integration**
- Sync external activities from Strava automatically
- AI-generated Strava post descriptions after workouts

**Calendar & Program Management**
- Visual program calendar showing the full training schedule
- Admin tools for program creation and management

## 4. Target Audience

**Primary:** HYROX competitors at all levels — from first-timers to seasoned racers.

**Segments:**
- **Beginners** — Gym-experienced athletes entering their first HYROX event, overwhelmed by the specificity of the training required
- **Intermediate Athletes** — 6+ months of HYROX training, looking to break through plateaus and race smarter
- **Advanced / Competitive Athletes** — Chasing podium finishes and personal bests, needing periodised, high-volume programming
- **Doubles & Relay Teams** — Athletes training for partner and relay formats who need synchronised preparation
- **Hybrid Fitness Enthusiasts** — People who love the crossover of running and strength training even outside of formal HYROX competition

**Geography:** Primarily UK-based (GBP pricing), with a global HYROX audience in mind.

**Psychographic Profile:**
- Driven, goal-oriented, performance-minded
- Values data and accountability
- Proud of their fitness identity
- Community-oriented (the HYROX community is tight-knit)
- Time-poor — needs structured, efficient training
- Willing to invest in tools that give them a competitive edge

## 5. Subscription & Pricing

- **Price:** £5/month
- **Trial:** 1-month free trial for all new users (no payment required to start)
- **Model:** Monthly recurring subscription via Stripe
- **Flexibility:** Users can pause or cancel at any time; access continues until the end of the paid period
- Admin accounts have full access independent of subscription status

The low price point (£5/month) is a deliberate positioning decision — making elite-level HYROX coaching accessible and reducing the barrier to entry for committed athletes who might otherwise pay hundreds for a human coach.

## 10. Important Context for AI-Generated Comms

When generating marketing copy, emails, or communications on behalf of HYBRIDX:

1. **Always anchor to HYROX specificity** — general fitness messaging undermines the brand's differentiated position
2. **Reference the £5/month price and free trial** when relevant — these are key conversion drivers
3. **Use performance language** — athletes respond to outcomes: faster times, better race positions, fewer blow-ups on the final run
4. **Acknowledge the community** — HYROX athletes are proud of their sport; respect and celebrate that identity
5. **Be concise and direct** — athletes don't want waffle; get to the point, make it punchy
6. **The AI coach is called the "Edge Coach"** — use this name when referencing the AI assistant feature
7. **Programs are 12 weeks** — mention this when relevant to set clear expectations
8. **The trial is 1 month, no card required** — lead with this to reduce friction
9. **HYROX (the sport) is always written as "HYROX"** in caps — respect the brand spelling
10. **The company/app is "HYBRIDX"** — not "HybridX", "Hybrid X", or "Hybrid-X" in formal contexts; the stylised form is HYBRIDX or HYBRIDX.CLUB
